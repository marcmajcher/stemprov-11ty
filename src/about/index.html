Hi! I'm Nic Bennett.

I research inclusion and belonging in science communication as a doctoral candidate in the Science & Technology Communication Lab of The University of Texas at Austin. My work lies at the intersection of science communication, applied theatre, and social justice. I use arts-based, participatory action research, and mixed methods to engage early-career scientists on issues of inclusion and belonging. Alongside scientists, artists, activists, and community members, I investigate how science communication spaces might become healing, liberatory spaces for all.
