Hi! I'm Nic Bennett.

I research power, ideology, and belonging in science communication as a postdoctoral researcher on the SciComm Identities Project at Michigan State University. I engage arts- and science-based research and practice to critique, disrupt, and reimagine science communication spaces. Alongside scientists, artists, activists, and community members, I hope to expand the circle of human concern in science communication and STEM.
